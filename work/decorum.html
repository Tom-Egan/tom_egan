Completed as part of a class assignment at the University of Wisconsin-Whitewater, Decorum is a fictional high-end furniture boutique. Project requirements included the conceptualization of a furniture company and develop a website relevant to their target audience. The demo features several pure CSS experiments relating to dropdown and button animation.
